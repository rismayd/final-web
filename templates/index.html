Providing Avenues to realize your talent

Talent network utililzes a blog to provide information from the global talent industry that is both informative and educational when it comes to helping you realize your talent. In addition, the website features a single news feed social network that allows you to send out message blasts to anyone on the talent network. You can ask questions to like minded users or post your accomplishments as a actor, model, singer, or dancer!

About Talent Network

Talent Network is an extension of TN Model and Talent and was developed for all members of the model and talent industry. The primary focus of Talent Network is to provide a space where models, actors, singers, and dancers can get access to industry news and advice from talent managers in the Greater Toronto Area. This website also has a focus on educating and nurturing young men and women who are interested in the industry and becoming a part of it.
