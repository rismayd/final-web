About TN Model and Talent

TN Management was developed by a team of international talent directors, each with experience in developing, managing, and placing talent with major national international clients. We specialize recruiting and connecting talented Models, Actors, Singers and Dancers with clients in Film, TV, Print, Music Video Choreography, Theater, Recording Industry and much more. TN Management is a small, innovative, and selective boutique style agency which provides national and international clients with unique talent.

TN Management ensures the success of all talent by including training sessions before each audition or go-see. By providing these free sessions, our talent is always able to confidently walk into every audition.

TN Management welcomes talent of all ages and experience levels to audition. If we do not feel you are ready for representation, we will take the time to point you in the right direction in terms of what you need so we can consider you at a later time. TN Management is not a school and will not teach you how to act, sing, dance, model, or play your instrument. Due to the competitive nature of the industry, we ask prospective talent to respect our Director decision if they suggest to seek further development before auditioning in the future.
